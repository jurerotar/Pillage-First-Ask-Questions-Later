# Introduction

<section>
  ## What is Pillage First!?

  **Pillage First! (Ask Questions Later)** is an open-source, single-player
  strategy game heavily inspired by Travian. You build a small village, grow it
  into a city, train an army and compete with NPC players living in the same
  game world — all at your own pace, fully offline if you want, with no
  subscriptions, no ads and no pay-to-win mechanics.

  Under the hood the game runs entirely on your device. That means your save is
  yours, you can back it up, and the game keeps working even when you go
  offline.
</section>

<section>
  ## What is the goal?

  There is no single "win condition" like you'd find in a match-based game.
  Instead, **Pillage First!** is about long-form progression:

  * Grow a single village into a capital-class economy.
  * Expand to multiple villages and manage them as a network.
  * Build an army and use it — to raid, defend, and assert control over a
  region of the map.
  * Complete quests, run hero adventures and chase high-tier artifacts.

  How you prioritise these is up to you. You can play peacefully and focus on
  building a beautiful economy, or lean into the game's name and go straight
  for your neighbours.
</section>
