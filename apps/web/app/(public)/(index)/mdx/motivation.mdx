I've always been a huge fan of **Travian** and similar browser-based strategy games. The slow, methodical village-building, resource management, and real-time progression made those games incredibly special.
<br />
Sadly, **Travian** requires a massive time (and gold!) investment to remain competitive. Not being able to commit either of these, I set out to create a similar, but less commitment-hungry experience, with more quality-of-life features and customizability to make your experience better!
<br />
**Pillage First!** is my attempt to recreate that experience as a **single-player Travian alternative**, with the same core mechanics, but modernized, **offline-friendly**, and fully **open source**, with no game mechanics to incentivize spending real-world money. Just the classic gameplay loop, reimagined for **solo play**.
<br />
Whether you're looking for a **Travian offline** experience or a **Travian single player** adventure, **Pillage First!** aims to provide that classic strategy feel without the pressure of 24/7 competition. As a **Travian open source** project, it's built by the community, for the community.
